Welcome to the Irregular Verbs Trainer

This is a simple application designed to help learn and practice irregular verbs.

It's possible to add new verbs and edit existing ones. You can choose between only practicing one, two or all three forms at the same time. There is a simple spaced repetition feature and on the list of verbs you can look up how well you know the forms for each.
